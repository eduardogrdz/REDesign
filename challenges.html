Challenge 1

Team Building and Planning
Challenge 1

The team Building and Planning challenge required members to not only break the ice, but also begin the early stages of the city planning like City Name, and individual roles within the members depending on their weaknesses and strengths.

After a brainstorming session, the team created the logo which is currently in place for our firm, embarked on our social media journey through Instagram and Twitter, developed the mission, vision, and slogan for the firm and established potential customers and products offered. Some of the products included education (in the form of professional development, children, etc.), employment through prioritized training, and leisure activities, such as concerts and sporting events.
